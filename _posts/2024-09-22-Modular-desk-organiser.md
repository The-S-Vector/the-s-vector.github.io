## Modular Desk Organsier
{: data-toc-skip='' .mt-4 .mb-0 }

I created this for my GCSE Design and Technology project. As the name states it is a desk organiser which uses allows the user to customize it however they want due to its modularity. It is mostly made from red and black PLA plastic, although the phone holder is made of steel. I will include the file at the bottom. 

The current parts include:
baseboard 
: the large board with the long rail slits

- hexagonal pencil holder 
- simple divider 
- phone stand
- headphone stand 
- key stand
- simple holders
